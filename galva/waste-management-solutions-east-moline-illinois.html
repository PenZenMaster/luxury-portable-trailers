Waste management is a multifaceted issue, encompassing everything from the collection and disposal of household garbage to the recycling of industrial waste. In East Moline, local authorities and private companies are working together to develop innovative solutions that address these challenges. One of the key strategies in the city's approach is the emphasis on recycling and composting. By encouraging residents and businesses to recycle paper, plastics, metals, and organic waste, East Moline aims to reduce the amount of waste sent to landfills significantly.
